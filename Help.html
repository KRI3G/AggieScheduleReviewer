Help Page

Step 1: Log in to Howdy

Begin by visiting this link and signing in with your Texas A&M User name and Password.

Step 2: Go to your dashboard

Navigate to the dashboard section of your Howdy portal.

Step 3: Print the schedule

Click on the print icon as shown, then click on export as PDF, and submit the PDF to the webpage!

Step 4: Upload your schedule

Once you have the PDF, go to the upload page and submit your schedule. Make sure to double-check that your file has been uploaded successfully.
